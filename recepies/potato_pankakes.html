Potato Pankakes

Description

Get ready to experience the ultimate crispy comfort food with classic Potato Pancakes. Known lovingly as deruny across Eastern Europe, these golden-brown fritters are a masterclass in transforming the humblest of root vegetables into an irresistible, savory treat. The secret lies in the perfect grate of starchy potatoes and sharp onions, bound together lightly and pan-fried until the edges are spectacularly crunchy while the centers remain fluffy and tender. The mouthwatering aroma of them sizzling in the skillet is usually enough to gather everyone in the kitchen before the first batch is even done.
The true beauty of potato pancakes is their incredible versatility, serving as a blank canvas for a variety of delicious toppings. Traditionally, they are enjoyed piping hot straight from the pan, crowned with a cool, generous dollop of rich sour cream and perhaps a sprinkle of fresh dill. For a heartier meal, they are phenomenal when smothered in a robust, earthy mushroom sauce or paired with crispy bacon. Whether you are serving them up for a cozy weekend breakfast or a comforting side dish, this recipe will help you achieve that flawless, crispy-on-the-outside, melt-in-your-mouth perfection every single time.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 medium-sized potatoes (about 300 g)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 tbsp sunflower oil for frying
  • sour cream for serving

Steps

  1. Peel 3-4 potatoes and an onion. Cut the vegetables into pieces and place them in a blender bowl.
  2. Додайте яйце та перебийте до однорідної маси. Перекладіть картопляно-цибулеву масу в миску, додайте 1 ст. л. борошна, посоліть та поперчіть до смаку та одразу починайте смажити деруни.
  3. Heat a frying pan with sunflower oil and spoon the potato mixture into it. Fry on both sides until golden brown. Place the finished deruny on a double layer of paper towel to absorb excess oil. Serve the deruny with sour cream.
